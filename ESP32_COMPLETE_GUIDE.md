# 🔌 ESP32 Complete Setup Guide

## Overview

This guide will help you connect your ESP32 microcontroller to control real home appliances like lights, fans, and outlets.

## ⚠️ Safety Warning

**DANGER: Working with AC mains voltage (110V/220V) can be LETHAL!**

- Only proceed if you have electrical experience
- Always turn OFF power at the breaker before wiring
- Use proper insulation and enclosures
- Consider hiring a licensed electrician
- Test with low voltage (5V/12V) devices first
